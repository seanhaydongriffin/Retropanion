Troubleshooting


Problem: Host "retropie" does not exist or FATAL ERROR: Network error: Connection timed out

Solution: Your RetroPie may be turned off.  Try turning on your RetroPie, waiting several seconds for the RetroPie to boot up, and repeat the action again.

You may not have SSH configured. If you are unsure if SSH is configured then check Appendix A for instructions on how to configure this.  Retropanion requires a SSH connection to your RetroPie to work.
